About MSU Denver's 50th Anniversary

For 50 years MSU Denver has transformed the lives of students, served as a courageous leader in higher education and boldly advanced the well-being of metro Denver communities and the state economy. Join the yearlong celebration of Colorado’s preeminent public urban University.
